# 文法
- ## 第四課
  - 由形容詞子句(relative clause)簡化而來
  - 稱作分詞片語，用於形容前方名詞
  - "The man wearing the yellow armband is the captain of that team"
  - ### 轉化步驟
    - 1. 去除關代和助動詞，若是否定句則加上not
    - 2. 動詞轉分詞: 主動ing, 被動用p.p.
    - 3. 省略beVerb，但是留下後方的分詞
    - 4. 保留非限定用法的逗號
    - 關代原句: "The taxi driver, who didn't realize   one of his tires had gone flat, continued driving."
    - 分詞片語: "The taxi driver, not        realizing one if his tires had gone flat, continued driving."
  - 特色: 簡潔有力
- ## 第五課
  - 前面子句視為單數，因此用"is"，用於強調"is"後的資訊
  - (All (that)/What) + S + (want to/need to/have to/can/should) + do is (to) VR
  - "In a fire, what you have to do is (to) keep calm and try to escape."
  - "All Walt Disney wanted to do was keep trying."
- ## 第六課
  - 若將 with 放置於主句之前則須加上逗號
  - ... + with + O + Ving/p.p./adj/prep ...
  - 現代分詞表主動，過去分詞表被動，也可加(adj)/介係詞(片語)
  - "Amy is speaking(,) with her body shaking and her eyes full of tear."
  - "Johnny walked out oof  the restaurant with his hands in his pocket."
