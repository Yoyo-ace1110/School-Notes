dedicate 投入/奉獻/獻出
    He dedicated two years to mastering C++ and LLVM.
    This server has a dedicated GPU for training the AI model.
    For 18 months, this author has dedicated himself to writing a book 
    about the snacks available in Taiwanese night markets.

distinguish 分辨
    As an art expert, Yoyo can easily distinguish a real painting from a copy.
    It is difficult to accurately distinguish between genuine antiques and clever fakes.

accurate 精準的/精確的
    Yoyo had his watch fixed because it was not accurate in telling time.
    A professional taster can distinguish expensive wine from cheap ones.

indicate 指出, indication 跡象
    A sudden drop in temperature is usually an indication that a storm is approaching.
    Recent research clearly indicates that only 3% of the children 
    in this country get as much daily exercise as they should.

reluctant 不情願的
    He was reluctant to leave the party because he was having so much fun.
    John reluctantly agreed to tidy his bedroom after his mother had asked him for the fourth time.

desperate 拚命的
    The hikers were lost in the mountains and desperately needed water.
    The little girl was desperate for her parents' attention, so she kept 
    dropping spoons and forks on the floor.

trace 痕跡/追蹤
    Despite their efforts, the police found no trace of the stolen jewelry.
    The family's history can be traced back to the 18th century.
