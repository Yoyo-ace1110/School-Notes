## 文法
- ### L7
  - S + 使役動詞 + O + p.p + ... 
- ### L8
  - The + (comparative) + S + V, the + (comparative) + S + V + ...
  - #### comparative
    1. adj變化(修飾名詞): (adj + er/more adj)
    2. adv變化(修飾動詞): (adv + er/more adv)
    3. 本身就是N: (more + N)
  - 有時 S + V 可省略 (慣用語)
- ### L9
  - #### 規則: 
    - 去掉連接詞, 主詞只出現一次
    - 若有否定助動詞(didn't...), 改成not
    - 主要動詞改分詞
    - 現在分詞(Ving) -> 主動
    - 過去分詞(p.p.) -> 被動
  - #### 分詞構句1: S + V + ... , (Ving/p.p) + ...
  - **例句:**
    - 原句:   Dave kept checking his smartphone in class, and he didn't look at his teacher even once.
    - 修改後: Dave kept checking his smartphone in class, not looking at his teacher even once.
  - ##### 分詞構句2: (Ving/p.p) + ... , S + V + ...
  - **例句:**
    - 原句:   Because Jason felt nervous about the final exam, he couldn't fall asleep the night before.
    - 修改後: Feeling nervous about the final exam, Jason coundn't fall asleep the night before.
